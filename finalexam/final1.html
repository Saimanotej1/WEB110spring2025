What is Pop Culture?

Pop culture, or popular culture, includes the ideas, perspectives, attitudes, memes, images, and other phenomena that are within the mainstream of a given culture. These include music, TV shows, movies, fashion, and celebrities that shape how society behaves and communicates.

Why Pop Culture Matters

Pop culture helps bring people together and create a shared sense of identity. It influences how we dress, talk, and interact with the world around us.
